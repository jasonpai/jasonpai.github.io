Pragmatic and agile fullstack enterprise development.

While I did mostly UI and gfx development during my studies and often stayed close to the hardware, starting my career I switched to jee based enterprise-backends. I worked in projects of various business contexts before I found my focus in ecommerce integration.

With the advent of robust browser programming environments I took interest in mastering the client side of things again.

After I managed to integrate my old rendering and eventdriven knowledge with my expertise in integration and data driven approaches, I became a fullstack developer.

My passion is integrating those seemingly opposite schools of thought into a great way of solving my customers requirements - finding pragmatic solutions in complex environments.
